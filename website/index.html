Created at PennAppsW2015 by Reed Rosenbluth, Jonathan Dubin, Tom Catullo, and Alex Crits-Christoph.

Corporate branding and advertisements are ubiquitous in society today and impossible to avoid. What if we lived in a world where consumers were blind to the excesses of corporate branding? Brand Killer is a technology demonstration of a future world in which consumers can empower themselves using augmented reality to literally ignore corporate influence. We built a head mounted display which uses openCV image processing to recognize and block brands and logos from the user's point of view in real time. It's AdBlock for Real Life.
